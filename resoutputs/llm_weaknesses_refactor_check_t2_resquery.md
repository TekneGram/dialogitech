## Synthesized Summary

The provided evidence does not directly address how Chain-of-Thought (CoT) impacts the accuracy of CEFR-generated text. However, research indicates that controlling linguistic features across different CEFR levels is challenging; for example, ChatGPT struggles to consistently match CEFR levels, with outputs tending to be either overly simple or overly difficult (Uchida, 2025).

Regarding CoT, some findings suggest that it enhances multi-step reasoning tasks and provides benefits across most long-context scenarios, increasing with context length (Zhu et al., 2025). Conversely, introducing CoT prompting may create interference for simpler tasks (Zhu et al., 2025). Furthermore, the effectiveness of CoT varies by model size, as smaller models may not show consistent gains (Zhu et al., 2025).
